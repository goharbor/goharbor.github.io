Our new Harbor site is up and running, and we're excited to get feedback.

Hello world!

We’ve been hard at work on our new Harbor site, and we’re thrilled to finally open it up to the world!

For those unfamiliar with Harbor, it is an open source cloud native registry with a focus on storing, scanning and securing content. Born out of a need for such a project at VMware, the registry’s roadmap and feature velocity is influenced by our community of users and developers.

As time progresses we’ll move most of the docs on our GitHub wiki to this site. Of course, our source code and even the source for this website will continue to live on GitHub and we welcome contributions of any kind.

Looking forward to seeing how Harbor grows as we continue on this open source journey.

Harbor Team
